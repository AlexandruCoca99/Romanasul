Prizes and Distinctions:

Since 1979, we have received the title of Laureate in every edition of the student’s folkloric festivals that we have taken part in, and of important cultural meanings in Europe, North America, Africa and Asia. We have won 36 First Prizes, 12 Second Prizes, 2 Third Prizes, 3 Gold Medals, 2 Silver Medals, and One Bronze Medal, along with numerous Special Prizes, Honorary Plaquettes and Diplomas, Excellence Diplomas, Merit Diplomas.
